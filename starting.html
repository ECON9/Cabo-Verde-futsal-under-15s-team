Player Profiles

Goalkeeper

#1 Mark Vozhina

Role: Shot-stopper & Swift Outlets

Commands the penalty area efficiently, offering lightning-fast distributions to launch instant counter-attacks.

Left Defender

#4 Ethan Costa

Role: Ball-winning Sweep

Composed under pressure, excels at timing physical tackles and turning defense into immediate transitions.

Right Defender

#69 Harris Borges

Role: Defensive Anchor

The structural organizer of our backline. Exceptional at intercepting aerial long balls and covering gaps.

Left Midfielder

#16 Oliver Semedo

Role: Flank Winger

Brings electric pace down the left touchline, combining tight dribbling skills with dangerous cut-back crosses.

Central Midfielder

#8 Lewis Paulo

Role: Engine Room Box-to-Box

Provides tireless stamina through the center, linking play between the backline and the final attacking third.

Right Midfielder

#19 Dylan Livermento

Role: Creative Playmaker

Elite 1v1 flair with high football intelligence. Carries a massive long-range shooting threat from outside the box.

Striker

#7 Liam Cabral

Role: Target Man

A highly physical forward who excels at holding up play under pressure and turning half-chances into clinical goals.
